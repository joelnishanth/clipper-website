How it works

Like Apple Notes, but it also transcribes your meeting — locally

1

Take notes during the call

Write bare notes the way you already do. Clipper runs quietly on your Mac — no bot joins the meeting.

2

Transcribe from your Mac's audio

Clipper captures your computer's audio directly and builds a local transcript with speaker labels and timestamps.

3

Enhance when the meeting ends

On-device AI merges your notes with the transcript into structured, searchable meeting notes — stored only on your machine.

No meeting bots

Works on Zoom, Meet, and Teams — without joining your call

Clipper transcribes your Mac's audio directly. Nothing enters the meeting as a participant. Detection is local; recording starts only when you tap Start.
